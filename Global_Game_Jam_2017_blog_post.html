Global Game Jam 2017 Super Smash Tokyo

We made a game at Global Game Jam 2017 called Super Smash Tokyo Using 3dpl as development tool. Sadale invited me to join GGJ while he was participating at the Hong Kong site. I invited Beljaxander to join me as a teammate, and I did not know if I could actually have my own team which I was going to lead… I did apply to be team leader on a kind of resume I filled out. finally when we were approaching the site we met with peter which wanted to join a group that could speak English. and we ended up being the only 3 foreigners at GGJ.
